Dear Friends,
Over the past few weeks, many of you have joined us in celebrating Bernhard Lederer's 40 years as an independent watchmaker through the stories, archives, and conversations that have shaped this anniversary.
Not every chapter of this anniversary is about presenting a watch. Some are simply about bringing people together.
Instead of inviting you to discover a new timepiece, we would simply like to invite you for a coffee.
For one day, Bernhard will step away from the workbench, put on a barista's apron, and personally prepare coffee for friends, collectors, journalists, partners, and neighbours in the heart of Geneva.
No presentations.
No speeches.
No appointments.
Just great coffee, meaningful conversations, and the pleasure of seeing familiar faces.
Date
Saturday
11 July 2026
Hours

– 7:00 PM
Location
Place de la Confédération
(in front of UBS), Geneva
If you happen to be in Geneva that day, we hope you'll stop by, enjoy a coffee, and spend a few moments with us.
We look forward to welcoming you.
Warm regards,
— The LEDERER Team
